Usa Project

Property Acquisition

Amayzin Grace Foundation International Inc. is proud to announce the acquisition of a new property in Akyem Anyinasin on August 23rd, 2020, with the vision of establishing a future orphanage. Our team firmly believes in the power of starting small and growing over time, as stated in Job 8:7. Our foundation is committed to sharing our gifts with the world and aims to utilize all available resources from God and generous donors to address societal issues and provide a safe haven for the homeless and abused. The future orphanage will serve as a place of excellence for the less privileged and abused members of our society, offering them the necessary support and guidance to lead fulfilling lives. We are dedicated to nurturing the orphans in our care and providing them with the tools to succeed in life. We believe that by investing in the next generation, we will reap a brighter future, and we are grateful to our donors for their unwavering support. As we embark on this project, we are inspired by the words of John Maxwell, "Those who pay the price reach their goal," and we are determined to make a positive impact on the lives of orphans and create a better tomorrow for them.
