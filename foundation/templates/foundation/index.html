    About Us

    The Amayzin Grace Foundation Intl Inc. was established by Grace Cece Ofosu, a compassionate and talented teacher, with the aim of creating a brighter future for the vulnerable, poor, and less-privileged individuals, particularly orphans and those who have been abused. Grace, the founder, was born in Ghana and raised in the United States, where she witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by orphans, abused individuals, and the less-privileged in accessing basic necessities such as food, education, and healthcare.

    Determined to make a difference, Grace is committed to partnering with philanthropists and donors to bring attention to the needs of neglected individuals, especially orphans and vulnerable children. She firmly believes that through collaborative efforts with donors, opportunities can be created to provide basic education, shelter, and healthcare support to those in need, particularly in developing countries.

    Grace's vision is rooted in the belief that investing in these individuals is not only an investment in their future, but also in the world as a whole, as it paves the way for the potential growth of future doctors and other professionals.

    USA Projects

    The foundation works diligently to provide emergency humanitarian assistance such as food, rental and utility assistance to low income communities in Anne Arundel County and the state of Maryland. Our diverse programs provides, motivates and empower young girls, to become empowered women and teaches self-esteem, youths in leadership training, entrepreneur skills, community building and providing medical knowledge and assistance to seniors and elderly in need

    School feeding, Scholarships to the girl child, and distribution of school supplies to under privileged children in Sierra Leone and promote education for girls.
